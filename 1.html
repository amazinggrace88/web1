How to spend your money


The say to spend your money is important. Because we always spend money to buy what we need or want. We can spend $100 at one day or one month. It is really up to you. The most important lesson is that spend your money less than what you earn. Therefore, if you want to spend a lot, you should earn a lot first.

The way we manage money is important in our life. These informations come from the book "The Simple Path to Wealth"
